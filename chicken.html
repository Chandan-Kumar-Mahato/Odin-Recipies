Sweet and Spicy Gochujang Chicken

Sweet and sour gochujang chicken is a quick wok dish featuring Korean flavors. Serve over rice.This dish takes 25 minute in total to serve to others , at one time we can serve this dish to our guest , friends.

Ingredients:

  • 1/3 cup gochujang (Korean hot pepper paste)
    1. Make the sauce in a bowl and cook the chicken in a wok.
  • 2 pounds skinless, boneless chicken thighs, cut into bite-size pieces
    1. Add the sauce to the wok (with the chicken) and cook until thick and bubbly.
  • 3 tablespoons thinly bias-sliced green onions
    1. Garnish and serve over rice.
  • 3 tablespoons thinly bias-sliced green onions
    1. Buy gochujang in the tube as it stays fresh longer and is a more manageable amount, recommends test kitchen expert Emily Nienhaus.
