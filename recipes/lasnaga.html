Italian Chicken Marinade

Its a Famous Chicken dish that is made in Italy , Milan. this dish takes 15 min to cook. In a small bowl, combine the extra virgin olive oil, balsamic vinegar, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried basil, dried rosemary, dried thyme, dried parsley, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to combine. Serve the Italian marinated chicken with your favorite side dishes, such as roasted vegetables, salad, or pasta.
